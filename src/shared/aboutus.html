About Us

Our Mission

Kawfi House is a committed team of talented individuals who provide superior service, superior coffees and teas, and freshly crafted pastries. Our goal is to provide all of our customers with an unparalleled product and experience. Our talented team of baristas and pastry chefs use the purist ingredients and cutting edge techniques to create a product that is truly superior in quality. We constantly provide outstanding customer service and believe that customer satisfaction is our ultimate goal. We are dedicated to provide our family of employees with a respectful and positive work environment where everyone is equal.
